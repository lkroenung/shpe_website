Welcome

SHPE is a social-technical organization whose primary function is to enhance and achieve the potential of Hispanics in engineering, math, and science. The Missouri S&T SHPE chapter engages in activities that develop its members' leadership skills, professional experience, and activities that increase community involvement in our vision.

Mission: SHPE is the Source for Quality Hispanic Engineers and Technical Talent.

News

  • 10-25-2014: SHPE Taco Sale Success!

    On October 22, 2014, SHPE opened up their first on-campus taco sale. The sale ran from 11 a.m. to 1 p.m. at The Puck, and it was a great success! Pairing with the delicious food was beautiful autumn weather. 

    At the taco sale customers were able to purchase a beef fajita taco and a refreshing bottle of Jarritos, a Mexican soda made with real cane sugar. Customers were also invited to top their taco with fresh pico de gallo. The beef itself was farm fresh, and very well cooked. Soon the food was sold out. Don't worry, this isn't the last taco sale.

    If you missed the recent taco sale, stayed tuned for more updates on the Missouri S&T SHPE Facebook Page and watch out for flyers around the S&T campus. 

    Also, keep an eye out for the SHPE Latin Night events, which are hosted with the Salsa Club.
